Biography


I am now a Quantitative Researcher of Zhonggeng Fund Co.,Ltd, Shanghai. Before that, I was a Senior Algorithm Engineer of Tencent Inc., CDG.
I received the B.Sc. degree in University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in June 2016. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in June 2022, under the supervision of Prof. Jingping Bi at Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ICT, CAS).
